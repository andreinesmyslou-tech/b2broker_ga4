How to read this The largest "Lost Reason" bucket points to the most actionable systemic problem. "Operational / Administrative Closure" usually means the deal died for non-business reasons (compliance, doc issues, internal process). "Budget Constraints" / "Selected Competitor" point to pricing or positioning. "No Decision / Status Quo" suggests sales-cycle pressure or weak business case. Disqualified-by-AI-Score shows how many leads the AI screen catches as SPAM/COLD; if (none) is the largest bucket, AI scoring is not consistently filled. Disqualified-by-Prior-Status shows where in the funnel they fell out.
